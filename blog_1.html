The general concept of this project is to take the concept of star formation and convert it to internet activities on social media. Like the formation of stars, dust and gas are compressed to a threshold until its explodtion. A topic before it is trending on internet, it has been incubating, compressing until it is "big" enough that can beb picked up by the traffic and eventually it dies out in the internet time strim. The project will be a artistic interpreation of internet activities.
